StackHack

Santa's patent present stacking machine has malfunctioned and is shooting out presents randomly instead of stacking them neatly. Unless the presents are stacked properly Santa will never get all his deliveries finished by Christmas morning. As Head Elf, it's your duty to help Santa by stacking the presents as shown by the holographic guides. Be quick - you only have a limited amount of time to stack each pile.

Guides with a question mark will accept any type of present. Other guides will only accept the type of present shown by the guide

Controls:

Move Left: left arrow

Move Right: right arrow

Jump: up arrow

Pick up/drop a present: space
